Tom Garrett

The Legislator Virginia Needs

  • Family Life and Education

    Tom was born in Atlanta to American parents. Tom studied Law at the University of Richmond, and currently lives in Scottsville, VA with his wife and two kids.

  • Military Service

    Tom is an experienced military veteran. During his six years of service, Garrett was a platoon leader, and led over 50 soldiers in the field. Garrett was honored for his service with the Army Commendation Medal and Army Achievement Medal on multiple occasions, as well as the NATO Medal

  • Political Experience

    Tom Garrett currently represents the 22nd district in the Virginia Senate. As such, Tom has experience in government fighting for the issues about which Americans care most, including immigration and gun rights.
